  Are you ready for a life-changing career at Novo Nordisk?

Working at Novo Nordisk is never just a job, it is an opportunity to have a life-changing career – you grow professionally and you impact the lives of our patients for better. We are looking for a Talent Acquisition Partner to support two affiliates in Region LATAM, Argentina and Chile & Perú.

About The Department

Anchored in our Global Talent Acquisition Centre of Excellence (Global TA CoE), our high-performing TA team consists of 5 dedicated TA professionals. Being anchored in the Global TA CoE under ‘TA International’, means that you will join a global team of functional subject matter experts in the area of TA and will therefore be able to tap into global TA best practice, which you will then be able to apply locally in Argentina, Chile & Perú. The position will be located in our office in Vicente López, Buenos Aires.

Argentina affiliate is located in Buenos Aires with approx. 150 employees. Chile & Perú affiliate has offices in Santiago de Chile and Lima, Perú with approx. 90 employees.

Together we are driving change with the aim of defeating diabetes and other serious chronic diseases such as obesity and rare blood and metabolic disorders.

The position

As our TA Partner for Argentina, Chile & Perú, you will be responsible for recruitments at all levels, which will give you the opportunity to collaborate with colleagues and business leaders across functions. You will be the TA subject matter expert, advising our hiring managers in all matters related to recruitment. You will be responsible for understanding the talent needs of the business and converting them into effective talent strategies. You will be also responsible for articulating all the activities between all stakeholders involved in the recruitment process, including Sourcer and Administrative. In addition, you will be responsible for facilitating the recruitment process by conducting candidate assessments and business cases when applicable. Ultimately, you will be responsible for delivering smooth and professional candidate and hiring manager experience during the entire recruitment process.

You will also be driving the Employer Branding activities in Argentina, Chile & Perú to ensure that Novo Nordisk is a preferred company to work for. You will be responsible for implementing innovative digital recruitment practices by using various social media platforms. You will also have a role to play in other TA related activities such us graduate recruitment activities.

As Talent Acquisition Partner, you will report to the Head of Talent Acquisition LATAM. You should expect some travel throughout the year in order to ensure a good coverage and collaboration with both affiliates.

Qualifications
You have minimum 4 years of experience from a similar TA role in a global company.
You have a bachelor’s degree within HR or a related field.
Fluency in English & Spanish is a must, and you have strong communication and presentation skills.
Experience with Employer Branding and effective usage of social media platforms.
Experience from an RPO (Recruitment Process Outsourced) supported recruitment delivery model is an advantage.
As a person you are ambitious, proactive and have strong stakeholder and project management skills with a proven track record of influencing with an inclusive style and ability to interact effectively with stakeholders at all levels, while you contribute to build a healthy work environment within the team, sharing perspectives and helping others.

799 millones fueron la cantidad de vidas impactadas por nuestros productos en 2019. Y si bien estamos orgullosos de eso, en este mundo de transformación digital y tecnológica también debemos preguntarnos: ¿cómo podemos continuar mejorando y llegar todavía a más vidas?

Tus responsabilidades:
Incluyen pero no están limitadas:
Realizar actividades de contabilidad más complejas, de acuerdo con los principios contables y normas para el control de los recursos financieros de la organización y asegurarse de que cumple con todas las normas, leyes y requisitos de presentación de informes;
Conciliar las cuentas más complejas y comprobar la exactitud de los asientos de diario para asegurarse de que el libro mayor esté preciso y completo;
Comprobar y analizar la información financiera y preparar los informes financieros más complejos y estados contables, de forma que la alta dirección tenga información precisa y oportuna para la toma de decisiones financieras, estos informes pueden incluir declaraciones de pérdidas y ganancias, balances generales, estados de cuenta de depreciación, flujo de efectivo, deudores y acreedores, las listas de planes de amortización, informes y presentaciones legales;
Proporcionar ayuda a los gerentes en los presupuestos de ingresos y gastos, comprender la información financiera y administrar sus responsabilidades financieras;
Entrenar personal de contabilidad con menos experiencia y ayudarles a resolver los problemas.

Requisitos mínimos
Egresado de Licenciatura con título Universitario en Contaduría, Finanzas, Administración o afín; Ingles Avanzado (escrito, lectura y oral);
Experiencia de al menos 4 años en el área contable para elaboración y revisión de información, elaboración de balances contables mensuales y anuales, solicitud de información a las diferentes áreas de manera local, regional y global; manejo de inventarios y activos fijo, soporte administrativo en ventas;
Trabajo y revisión de información con despachos contables externos; manejo de normas contables locales y globales; experiencia previa idealmente en el sector farmacéutico o con empresas del sector farmacéutico; capacidad de desarrollo en un ambiente analítico y ser multitask para tener crecimiento profesional dentro de la empresa;
Sólidos conocimientos en SAP FICO, Excel y Power Point;
Tener buenas relaciones interpersonales y capacidad para interactuar con otras áreas; Tener excelentes habilidades de comunicación, para influenciar, persuadir y negociar con los Equipos de Trabajo y los Stakeholders; Tener altos estándares de Ética e Integridad; Tener como objetivo personal y profesional el ayudar a la gente a través de la Excelencia Operativa; Tener excelentes habilidades para buscar y promover la Mejora Continua.

Recibirás:
Salario competitivo, bono anual, esquema flexible de trabajo, comedor subsidiado, programa de reconocimiento, desarrollo profesional, capacitación continua, diversidad e inclusión.

Medical scientific liaison (MSL) programs are expanding in today’s pharmaceutical industry. MSLs are therapeutic specialists with advanced scientific training and degrees in life sciences (careers related to sciences such as medicine, biology, pharmacy, etc). MSL programs have an integral place in the life-cycle development of a product within the rheumatology area.

Prominent field-based medical programs are implemented in most pharmaceutical companies. Many companies also employ MSLs as key sources of contact with specialist for a therapeutic area. This is very important in the case of Pfizer taking into account the forthcoming products in the oncology area which are in development (phase II and III). Detailed Description Below

Primary responsibilities include medical and safety oversight of clinical trials as well as corporate support for business opportunities. The Medical Director provides Medical Monitoring services to clinical trials with the aim to monitor the safety and well being of study participants, integrity of the project data in accordance with the clinical trial protocol/clinical study protocol (or equivalent documents), standard operating procedures (SOPs), good clinical practice (GCP), and all other applicable directives and regulatory requirements. Responsibilities include medical supervision of assigned projects from both a medical and scientific perspective. The Medical Director provides and/or oversees medical monitoring activities including protocol development, development and/or review of project-specific Medical Monitoring Plan (MMP), review of other project-specific plans, responding to medically related questions, answering Medical questions arising at any stage of a clinical trial, and maintaining medical oversight for the conduct of a clinical trial. Reviews of medical data and/or safety information may be a regular part of the position. The Medical Director acts as liaison between the Sponsor/Client who is responsible for medical decisions made in a project, the investigational sites and the project team.
